# v0.6.1 Process-to-Device Target Architecture

## Layered Architecture

| Layer | Name | Input | Output | Status | Release |
|-------|------|-------|--------|--------|---------|
| L0 | Process/thermal/defect | Process parameters | C_def(x,y) | ✅ Implemented | v0.3 |
| L1 | Interface energy profile | D_it(E) piecewise | N_it [m⁻²] | ✅ Implemented | v0.6.0 |
| L2 | Surface charge | N_it, f_occ, sign | σ_eff [C·m⁻²] | ✅ Implemented | v0.5/v0.6 |
| L3 | Volume charge mapping | σ_eff, geometry | ρ_eff [C·m⁻³] | 🔒 Blocked | v0.7 ADR |
| L4 | Electrostatics | ρ_eff, ε, BCs | φ(x,y) [V] | 🔒 Future | v0.8 |
| L5 | Confinement potential | φ(x,y) | V_conf(x) [eV] | 🔒 Future | v0.9 |
| L6 | Quantum confinement | V_conf, m_eff | ψ(x), E [eV] | 🔒 Future | v0.9 |
| L7 | Qubit metrics | ψ, E, noise model | T₁, T₂, fidelity | ❌ Blocked | Beyond MVP |

## Data Flow Diagram

```
L0: Process → C_def(x,y,t_final)
      ↓
L1: C_def → D_it(E) [piecewise bins]
      ↓
L1→L2: N_it = Σ D_i × ΔE_i
      ↓
L2: σ_eff = s × q_e × N_it × f_occ
      ↓
L3: ρ_eff = σ_eff / t_eff        [REQUIRES ADR-C2]
      ↓
L4: ∇·(ε∇φ) = −ρ_eff             [REQUIRES ADR-Poisson-coupling]
      ↓
L5: V_conf(x) = −q_e × φ(x)
      ↓
L6: (−ℏ²/2m_eff)∇²ψ + V_conf·ψ = E·ψ   [REQUIRES ADR-Schrödinger]
```

## Explicit Non-Goals

- ❌ Calibrated qubit simulation.
- ❌ T₁/T₂ prediction.
- ❌ Gate fidelity prediction.
- ❌ Industrial TCAD replacement.
- ❌ QTCAD replacement.
- ❌ Claim of reproducing Maurand device.
- ❌ Noise spectroscopy.
- ❌ Spin-orbit coupling.
- ❌ Hyperfine interaction.

## MVP v1.0 Definition

A successful v1.0 demonstrator must show:

1. **Traceable input:** D_it(E) profiles with documented evidence level.
2. **Charge mapping:** σ_eff → ρ_eff with explicit t_eff definition.
3. **Electrostatic potential:** φ(x,y) from Poisson with ρ_eff source.
4. **Confinement potential:** V_conf derived from φ.
5. **Schrödinger eigenstates:** ψ(x), E in simplified 1D/2D geometry.
6. **No calibration claims:** all results labeled as demonstrative.
7. **Full audit trail:** ADRs, councils, risk matrices for each layer.
