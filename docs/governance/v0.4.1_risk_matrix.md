# Risk Matrix — v0.4.1 Parameter Curation

> **Date:** 2026-05-10

## Risk Register

| ID | Risk | Likelihood | Impact | Mitigation |
|----|------|------------|--------|------------|
| R1 | **Premature calibration claim.** A reader interprets literature ranges as calibrated values in the code. | Medium | High | Every TX/T0 parameter has explicit `do_not_claim`. Decision brief states no calibration performed. |
| R2 | **Material stack confusion.** Parameters from wrong material system (SiC, GaN, HfO₂) applied to Si/SiO₂. | Low | High | Sources are classified by `material_stack`. No non-Si sources used for Si parameters. |
| R3 | **Unit mismatch.** Surface density (C/m²) confused with volume density (C/m³) during future calibration. | Medium | High | Unit audit section in literature review. t_eff role explicitly documented. |
| R4 | **Parameter tier inflation.** T0 parameters promoted to T2 without adequate literature support. | Low | Medium | Council deliberation requires source_id for any promotion to T2. |
| R5 | **Scope creep.** v0.4.1 documentation leads to unauthorized code changes. | Low | Medium | Acceptance gate G5 enforces zero src/scripts/tests changes. |
| R6 | **Literature staleness.** Cited values become outdated by newer measurements. | Low | Low | Registry records publication year. Periodic review recommended. |
| R7 | **Overclaim in README.** README update introduces language suggesting calibration. | Low | High | README changes reviewed against overclaim audit checklist. |

## Risk Heat Map

```
           Low Impact    Medium Impact   High Impact
```

## Residual Risk Assessment

All identified risks are mitigated by the documentation-only nature of
v0.4.1. The primary residual risk (R1) is addressed by explicit `do_not_claim`
fields in every TX parameter and the decision brief's clear statement that
no calibration is performed.
