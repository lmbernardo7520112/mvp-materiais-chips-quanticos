# v0.6.0 Energy Profiles Policy Activation

## Reason

`energy_profiles.py` and `test_energy_profiles.py` mention controlled terms
only as **negation guards** and scientific boundary declarations.

Examples of controlled terms in negation context:

- "No Poisson equation coupling." (docstring boundary declaration)
- `assert not hasattr(energy_profiles, "compute_rho_eff")` (scope test)
- `def test_no_rho_eff_no_t_eff_no_solver_coupling` (test name)

These are the same patterns already authorized for `surface_charge.py` and
`test_surface_charge.py` in the v0.5 stage.

## Decision

Authorize only the new v0.6.0 files in `policy.json` v0.5 stage
`authorized_files` list.

## Scope

- ✅ This does NOT remove any gate.
- ✅ This does NOT remove any forbidden term.
- ✅ This does NOT broaden the whitelist beyond the two new files.
- ✅ This does NOT authorize ρ_eff, t_eff, solver coupling, C2, or
  calibration claims.
