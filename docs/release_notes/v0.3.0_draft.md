## Summary

v0.3.0 implements the first material-physics layer of the MVP: a
defect-like state variable C_def(x,y,t) governed by thermally-activated
reaction-diffusion kinetics.

C_def is an **adimensional proxy** — not a calibrated physical defect
concentration.

- ❌ Schrödinger equation — deferred to v0.5

- ❌ Real charge noise prediction
- ❌ Quantum coherence / fidelity prediction

## Scientific Limits

- C_def is **adimensional** — not a physical concentration.
- All kinetic parameters are **toy/demonstrative** unless explicitly
  curated from literature (see `docs/parameters_v0.3_candidates.md`).
- The model does NOT predict: defect density, charge noise, coherence,
  fidelity, wafer quality, or device performance.
- Figures carry disclaimer: "demonstrative — not calibrated."

## Relation to ADR-006

ADR-006 (Accepted) established:

1. C_def as bounded, adimensional, defect-like proxy
2. Reaction-diffusion as the governing equation form
3. Nine strict guardrails (No Poisson, No Schrödinger, etc.)

All nine guardrails are satisfied in this release.
