# v0.6.1 — Process-to-Device Roadmap Re-Anchoring

## Summary

Re-anchors the project toward a process-to-device demonstrator for
silicon/CMOS qubits inspired by Maurand et al. 2016.

## Mission

> Build a process-to-device demonstrator for silicon/CMOS qubits to
> study how interface-defect and surface-charge profiles modify
> electrostatic potential and effective quantum confinement, without
> claiming experimental calibration or coherence prediction.

## Added

- ADR-011 Proposed: process-to-device qubit demonstrator roadmap.
- Strategy brief: 4 options compared, Option C selected.
- Research council: 8 specialists, 4 skeptical objections, 8/8 unanimous.
- Target architecture: 7-layer L0→L6 with units and data flow.
- Roadmap: v0.6.1→v1.0 with stop conditions.
- Risk matrix: 10 risks (6 Critical, 3 High, 1 Medium).
- Acceptance gates: 25 gates.

## Decision

Proceed layer-by-layer:

```
D_it(E) → N_it → σ_eff → ρ_eff → φ → V_conf → ψ, E
```
