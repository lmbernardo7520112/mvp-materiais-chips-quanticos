# v0.9.0 External Benchmark Readiness Brief

## Decision Question

Is the project ready to plan a future external numerical benchmark?

## Recommended Benchmark Path

* Option A first;
* Option B second;
* Option C later;
* Option D blocked until data source is identified.

## Why

Manufactured solution (Option A) permits testing numerical consistency and convergence without pretending experimental validation. It isolates the mathematical correctness of a future solver from physical truth claims.

## Not Yet Ready For

* experimental validation;
* calibrated device prediction;
* physical phi claims;
* coherence prediction.

## Future Required Evidence

* equation statement;
* boundary condition statement;
* unit convention;
* manufactured solution;
* error norm;
* convergence trend;
* reproducibility script;
* no calibration claim;
* no device prediction.
