# v0.5.1 C1 Validation Scale Brief

## Purpose

Explain that v0.5.1 demonstrates C1 numerical scale, not experimental calibration. The artifacts generated are for validating dimensional consistency, sign conventions, and monotonicity, not for physical predictions.

## Demonstration Parameters

- **D_it grid**: [1e10, 1e11, 1e12] eV⁻¹·cm⁻² (sweeping typical physical ranges)
- **delta_E_window grid**: [0.01, 0.05, 0.10] eV
- **f_occ grid**: [0.0, 0.25, 0.5, 1.0]
- **s_charge values**: [-1, +1] (donor and acceptor-like behavior)

## Expected Scaling

- **sigma_eff linear in D_it**: Charge magnitude scales linearly with defect density for a given energy window.
- **sigma_eff linear in delta_E_window**: Charge magnitude scales linearly with the energy window.
- **sigma_eff linear in f_occ**: Charge magnitude scales linearly with the fractional occupancy.
- **sign controlled by s_charge**: The sign of the resulting charge density matches the given sign convention.
- **zero occupancy gives zero charge**: Verified as a necessary physical boundary condition.

## Limits

- no rho_eff;
- no t_eff;
- no solver coupling;
- no physical phi;
- no device prediction;
- no calibration.
