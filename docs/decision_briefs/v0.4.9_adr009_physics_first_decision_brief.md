## Core Question

What is the smallest honest physics advance after v0.4.8?

The audit concluded: governance is mature (4.5/5), but the model remains
demonstrative. The next step must produce **dimensional physics content**,
not more process.

## Why C1 Is the Right Step

1. **Honest progress:** D_it → σ_eff is real physics with verifiable
   units, not just more metadata or documentation.
2. **No solver risk:** The Poisson solver stays untouched. No ε
   substitution, no convergence risk, no numerical stability concern.
3. **Testable without PDE:** Every step in the C1 chain can be tested
   with pure arithmetic (unit conversion, bounds, sign).
4. **Cleanly bounded:** C1 cannot accidentally produce physical φ
   because it never touches the solver.

## Why Not C0

C0 preserves safety but the audit explicitly warns: "the methodology
has hardened governance faster than science." More metadata-only releases
without dimensional content would validate this criticism.

## Why Not C2/C3 Now

C2 requires t_eff (regularization vs physical thickness), solver coupling,
and a benchmark (manufactured solution or analytical 1D capacitor). None of
these prerequisites exist. C3 additionally requires experimental validation.

## Decision

**C1 is the recommended next route.**

- ADR-009 Proposed with C1 scope.
- C2/C3 remain blocked.
- Implementation requires ADR-009 Accepted + TDD RED→GREEN.
- The project must stop creating additional governance-only releases and
  use the existing governance to advance physics.
