# v0.9.1 Manufactured Poisson Benchmark Decision Brief

## Question

Should the next benchmark strategy be a manufactured Poisson benchmark?

## Recommendation

Recommend ADR-017 as Proposed.

## Rationale

Manufactured solution benchmarks test numerical consistency without overclaiming experimental validation.

## Recommended Order

1. 1D manufactured benchmark.
2. 2D manufactured benchmark.
3. internal solver comparison.
4. external solver comparison.
5. experimental calibration only after real data source exists.

## Not Ready For

* physical phi claim;
* calibrated device response;
* coherence prediction;
* experimental validation.
