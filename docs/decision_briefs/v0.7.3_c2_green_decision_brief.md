# v0.7.3 C2 GREEN Decision Brief

## Context
ADR-012 has been accepted, authorizing the implementation of the C2 charge mapping phase exclusively through Interface Sheet Source and Conservative Volume Regularization.

## Decisions
1. **Primary Source Implementation**: `InterfaceSheetSource` is implemented as a dataclass to hold the effective sheet charge density (`sigma_c_per_m2`).
2. **Fallback Regularization**: `ConservativeVolumeRegularization` is implemented to handle numerical-only volume mapping (`rho_reg_c_per_m3` from `l_reg`), strictly maintaining charge conservation.
3. **Metadata Enrichment**: `DepthPriorMetadata` and `ExperimentalDepthPriorMetadata` are implemented to carry literature and experimental context without coupling to the solver.
4. **Solver & Coupling Strictness**: All tests verifying the absence of `poisson_solver_2d`, quantum confinement, calibration, and physical potential interpretations passed, guaranteeing C2 isolation.

## Technical Rules Enforced
- No solver imports.
- No physical `t_eff`.
- Charge conservation is explicitly validated.
- `calibration_status` is rigidly defaulted to `not_calibrated`.
