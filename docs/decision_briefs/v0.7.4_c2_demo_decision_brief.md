# v0.7.4 C2 Demo Decision Brief

## Context
v0.7.3 implemented C2 charge mapping (InterfaceSheetSource, ConservativeVolumeRegularization, DepthPriorMetadata) as isolated bookkeeping. Before proceeding to an ADR for solver coupling (v0.8.0), a demonstration release validates the C2 mapping numerically.

## Why v0.7.4 Before Solver Coupling
- Sanity checks reduce risk before coupling to the Poisson solver.
- Demonstrating charge conservation and l_reg sensitivity builds confidence.
- A visible CSV/figure trail proves the mapping works in isolation.

## Why No Poisson Yet
- ADR-012 authorized C2 mapping but not solver coupling.
- A future ADR (C3) must govern how sigma_eff or rho_reg feeds into the solver.
- Premature coupling risks false physical claims.

## Why l_reg Remains Numerical-Only
- l_reg is a regularization length, not a physical thickness.
- It controls the volume over which sheet charge is smeared for numerical stability.
- It carries no physical interpretation and is explicitly labeled `not_physical_t_eff=True`.

## Decision
Create isolated demo script + tests validating C2 conservation and sensitivity properties without any solver coupling.
